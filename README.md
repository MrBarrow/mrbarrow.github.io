# Privacy Policy for Aether AI Wallpaper

**Last Updated: December 2, 2025**

## Introduction

Jacob Barrow ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Aether AI Wallpaper mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our App.

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree with the terms of this Privacy Policy, please do not access the App.

## Information We Collect

### Account Information

When you create an account with Aether AI Wallpaper, we collect your email address through Firebase Authentication. You may create an account using:
- Email and password
- Google Sign-In (which provides us with your email address from your Google account)

We use this email address solely for:
- Account creation and authentication
- Account recovery
- Essential service communications related to your account

### Payment Information

If you choose to purchase credits or subscribe to our monthly subscription service, payment processing is handled by Google Play's billing system. We do not collect, store, or have access to your credit card or payment information. We only receive confirmation of successful purchases from Google Play.

### Wallpaper Generation Data

When you request AI-generated wallpapers, we may collect and transmit the following information to our AI service providers:
- Text prompts or descriptions you provide for wallpaper generation
- Settings and preferences related to wallpaper generation

This information is sent to third-party AI services (OpenAI and Runware) to generate your custom wallpapers.

### Automatically Collected Information

We do not collect device information, analytics data, or usage statistics beyond what is necessary for Firebase Authentication to function.

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect only for the following purposes:

- **Account Management:** To create, maintain, and authenticate your user account
- **Service Delivery:** To provide you with access to AI-generated wallpapers based on your subscription or credit purchases
- **Wallpaper Generation:** To send your prompts and preferences to AI service providers (OpenAI and Runware) to generate custom wallpapers for you
- **Transaction Processing:** To manage your purchases and subscriptions through Google Play billing
- **Customer Support:** To respond to your inquiries and provide technical support

## Data Storage and Security

Your account information is stored securely using Firebase Authentication services, which employs industry-standard security measures. We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

However, please note that no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## Third-Party Services

### Firebase Authentication

We use Google Firebase Authentication to manage user accounts and login functionality. Firebase may collect certain information to provide authentication services. For more information about how Google processes data, please review Google's Privacy Policy at https://policies.google.com/privacy

### OpenAI

We use OpenAI's services to generate AI wallpapers based on your text prompts and preferences. When you request a wallpaper, your prompt and generation settings are sent to OpenAI's servers for processing. OpenAI processes this information according to their privacy policy. For more information, please review OpenAI's Privacy Policy at https://openai.com/privacy

### Runware

We use Runware's services to generate AI wallpapers based on your text prompts and preferences. When you request a wallpaper, your prompt and generation settings are sent to Runware's servers for processing. Runware processes this information according to their privacy policy. For more information, please review Runware's Privacy Policy at https://runware.ai/privacy-policy

**Important Note:** The prompts and preferences you provide for wallpaper generation are sent to these third-party AI services. We recommend not including personal, sensitive, or confidential information in your wallpaper prompts.

### Google Play Billing

All in-app purchases and subscriptions are processed through Google Play's billing system. Google Play's privacy practices govern the processing of payment information. Please review Google's Privacy Policy for more information.
